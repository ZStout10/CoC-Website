About Us:

The Office is a friendly clan that hungers for competitive war. With experienced war leaders, assigned targets, multiple communication tools and the ability to teach new members strong attacks, our war win/loss ratio speaks for itself (shown at the bottom)! Our excellent strategies and the ability to donate the best level troops possible, we will help you become the best possible attacker for clan wars and CWL (Clan War Leagues).

Even though we are a very competitive war clan, we do not require you to be in every single clan war/CWL. Even if you prefer to sit on the side-lines and farm resources, you will still have access to the best donations possible!
